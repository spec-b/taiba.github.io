I'm interested in exploring automated formal reasoning, specifically formal verification of autogenerated code, automated proof search, automated program repair, and ensuring soundness in programming languages.

I will be working with Dr Borzoo Bonakdarpour at the TART lab, MSU, on type-directed program synthesis and applications of modal type systems.

Before MSU, I was at Carnegie Mellon University, studying Logic.

During my master's at CMU I got interested in learning more about Functional Programming, Type Theory (specifically Modal Type Theories, Substructural Type Theories) and Category Theory.

My undergrad was in Math (with a little bit of CS and Statistics) from Pondicherry University. As a math person, my interests lie in Algebra, specifically in Homological Algebra and Non-Commutative Ring Theory.
